Rules

Do

  • Keep the three items: ⌂ Home · Why Drylands · Library — in that order, on every page.
  • Keep the menu as <div class="db-nav"> — a div, never <nav>.
  • Preserve the ../ / ../../ href prefix on sub-folder pages.
  • Let dfsa-i18n.js inject the language switcher into .db-nav.

Don't

  • Use a <nav> element — a page-level nav{} rule bleeds a green box into the bar.
  • Re-add retired items (About, Research, Transparency, Community, MARVEL, partner CTA).
  • Hard-code a second static language switcher (i18n injects one).
  • Swap the clay fill back to the old forest green #1f523a.
