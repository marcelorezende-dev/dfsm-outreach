Spacing, radii & elevation

Layout rhythm sits on a 4px step; corners use four radii; depth uses a three-step warm shadow scale. All are tokens — compose with them instead of arbitrary values.

Spacing scale

4px base step (--sp-1 … --sp-8). Bars below are drawn at the real token width.

Corner radii

Four steps — small controls, medium cards, large panels, full pills.

--r-sm
6px · inputs, chips
--r-md
12px · cards
--r-lg
20px · panels, frames
--r-pill
999px · buttons, tags

Elevation

Warm, low-contrast shadows (tinted with the ink hue) in three steps.

--shadow-sm
chips, swatches
--shadow-md
cards on hover
--shadow-lg
popovers, modals
