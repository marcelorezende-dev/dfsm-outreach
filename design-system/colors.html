Foundations

Colour

The DFSA palette is drawn from the DSL-IP brand lockup and the split-globe illustration: warm terracotta and forest green over a speckled paper cream, with a small set of illustration-derived accents. Every colour is a CSS custom property in colors_and_type.css — always reference the token, never a raw hex.
