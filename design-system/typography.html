Typography

Three voices: a rough condensed CAPS face for large display, a humanist serif for reading, and a geometric sans for UI, labels and data. Families and the modular scale live as tokens in colors_and_type.css; apply them through the .dsl-* classes.

Families

Bundled offline as web-fonts. Set via --font-display, --font-serif/--font-body, --font-sans.
