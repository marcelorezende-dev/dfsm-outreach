01

What this pillar holds

Integrated Management is where assessment becomes action — the place the Programme turns evidence about a landscape into practices farmers can use.

It is anchored by the Sustainable Landscape Production Framework (SLPF), which combines three mutually reinforcing streams — Community Seed Banks, the Forest & Farm Facility and Farmer Field Schools — delivered as a coherent package rather than disconnected projects. Each stream works alone; their power is in integration.
